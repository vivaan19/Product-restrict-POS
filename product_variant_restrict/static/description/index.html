Product Variant Restrict in POS Screen

After installing the Product Restrict module, you have the ability to exclude counters for product variants in the point of sale. This feature allows you to streamline your sales process and make it more efficient.

Here's how you can utilize this functionality:

Configure Your Products:

Within the Sale module, access the product variants that you want to exclude counters for.This can be done by going to the Products -> Product Variants section.

Edit Product Variant Settings:

Once you've located the specific product variants, you'll need to edit their settings. Look for options by selecting the Product variant.

Disable Counters:

Odoo provides the option to disable counters for specific product variants. Select the counters you want to conceal for the product variant in the 'Ignore Counter' field.
